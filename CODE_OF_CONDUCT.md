# Code of Conduct

`pager` follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) (version 2.1).

In short:

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good faith from contributors and maintainers.
- Critique code, not people.
- Personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language are not welcome and will be moderated.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience something that violates the above, please open a private channel rather than calling it out publicly:

- Open a GitHub issue marked confidential, OR
- DM the maintainer on GitHub (@jawwadzafar).

The maintainer will follow up promptly and confidentially.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: this repo, the website at https://jawwadzafar.github.io/pager, and any channel where someone is acting as a `pager` contributor.

---

By contributing you agree to abide by this.
